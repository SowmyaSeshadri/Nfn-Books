TOOLS

Tools we use on a daily basis that enhance our design workflows.

Sketch

Sketch is our default design tool and our team loves it for its flexibility and plugins.

Figma

We love experimenting. We love Figma for it's real-time collaboration and comments system. Amazed by how fast it is being a web service.

Keynote

Keynote is not only for presenting our work but for quick interaction designs as well. Magic Move.#FTW

InVision

Our designs are shared via InVision along with clickble prototypes. Great feedback mechanism. Using the inspect feature as well(still in beta).

Zeplin

Zeplin,for us,serves as a bridge between designers and front-end developers and we love it.Thanks to perfect sketch integration.

Demonstrate

We brewed Demonstrate,the prototyping tool, so that we create native app demos without writing a single line of code.

Flinto

Flinto is also a part of our experimental crew which is pretty useful for making quick prototypes for web/mobile.

Principle

Our default interaction design tool at the moment.Easy to use,simple controls for animation and we love the screen recording feature.

Origami by Facebook

A relatively new native prototyping app which has a steep learning curve. We're still learning this and figuring it out.

Telegram

Telegram has been a part of NFN from the day it launched. Love the speed at which we can share cat GIFs.

Flock

Experimenting Flock for its inbuilt To-Do list shared document and polls.So far so good.

Slack

All client communication are via Slack with the Trello,Github and BitBucket integrations.
